Taxi pricing in NYC vs Mumbai

29 December 2013

Something else that struck me during my trip to India was the difference in taxi fare between New York City and Mumbai. I expected them to be different but the magnitude of the difference was shocking. In NYC, the base fare is $2.50 and increases 50 cents for each additional 1/5th of a mile or 60 seconds of not moving. In Mumbai, the rate starts at 19 rupees (~32 cents) and includes the first 1.5 km.
